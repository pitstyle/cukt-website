# Atomic Love

**Description:** 7:45, 2002, video, photography 100x100 cm, (with Illia Chichkan) *Atomic Love* is the image of a post-nuclear world. A couple of lovers wanders through the deserted site of the former Chernobyl nuclear plant in search of extreme experiences. Dressed in silver protective suits, they try to conduct a romantic walk through an area where radiation ranges between 50 -120 micro roentgen per hour. This is no longer a futuristic vision, this is science-fiction becoming science-reality. The project was shot in the closed zone of the Chernobyl nuclear plant.
