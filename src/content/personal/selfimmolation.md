# Selfimmolation

**Description:** App, installation, website, 2014, work in progress.

App, installation, website, 2014, work in progress.

Self - immolation Voluntary Kit - is used to carry out a public act of virtual self-immolation. The kit consists of a mobile application and a portable folding podium. Final demonstration can be done anywhere. To do this, spread the podium at the selected point and stand on it. Run the application, rise your hand above you and point the camera at yourself. Stand motionless and be proud having the moment of importance in mind. Photo of self-immolation will be triggered automatically and shared with people all over the world through selected social media. Final demonstration can be done only once a day. Be responsible, consciously choose the acting points in public spaces.

Exhibition: "Sobreexposition", MAC, Chille, 2016.
