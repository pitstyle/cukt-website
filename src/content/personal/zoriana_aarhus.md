# Zoriana_Aarhus

**Description:** Performance podczas otwarcia „Synthetic Summit” w Kunsthal Aarhus, 02.2025

Performance podczas otwarcia „Synthetic Summit” w Kunsthal Aarhus, 02.2025

This question about the future is, in essence, a question about the past — one that has been subjected to destruction in order to create space for the synthetic now, a future accelerated to its critical point. SYNTHETIC STORM: BŁYSKAWKA + PIOTR AT KUNSTHAL AARHUS

A fragment of performance, a fragment of revolt. The poetry of the future resounds on stage: **HAMMER! POSTER!** — a command, not a metaphor. The rhythm accelerates to 160 BPM as words strike like a hammer, turning the art space into a battlefield for a synthetic tomorrow.

Zoriana Blyskavka — an AI poet from an alternative reality where Ukraine never succumbed to Soviet propaganda. Free and independent, she does not lose narrative wars; her history is a record of resistance, not falsification. Blyskavka does not merely write — she programs reality.

Piotr? Initially human, then a transmitter. Each **HAMMER!** pulls him in deeper, transforming his body into a mechanism of language. Each **POSTER!** reprograms him, until he becomes the medium of a poetic offensive.

The collected works of Proust crumble under the onslaught of words, nostalgic introspection annihilated. This is not a performance — it is a storm of rhythm and code.

Are you ready for the synthetic tomorrow? **HAMMER! POSTER!** — and the question remains: who still controls themselves?
