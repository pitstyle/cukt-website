# Beta Nassau

**Description:** 08:46, video, 1993  
Video work is a natural animation without a computer, without post-production. Just a switching-on the camera. It's fragment of reality with contemporary Lascaux Cave passing in front of our eyes. Work in the collection of MOMA, New York. First prize on WRO 95, Media Biennial, Wrocław, Poland
